Completed Projects

FIRST Robotics Competition

International competitive robotics program, with thousands of teams competing in annual competitions.

As co-leader and electrical lead, I worked on mechanical and electrical design components of the robot, including the automatic launching mechanism, and the CAN signal network for electrical devices.

Custom Coffee Table

A custom-designed, never-before-seen, 5' x 2' live-edge, maple wood coffee table.

Utilized OnShape CAD software to produce comprehensive parametrized 3D model before manufacturing.

Arbitrary Function Generator

Custom-designed PCB to generate an arbitrary square wave between -5 V and +5 V.

Schematic constructed and developed using KiCAD. Hand-soldered and assembled.

Adaptive Device

Adaptive device concept to crack an egg for people with parapelegia.

Modeled using OnShape, and designed to be easily print-in-place using selective laser sinstering (SLS) manufacturing.

Machined Phone Holder

Machined phone holder produced from third-angle projection drawings using standard shop tools. Made out of brass and aluminum.

Bell AVR Simulatory Firefighting Drone

Bell Advanced Vertical Robotics is a competition simulating firefighting with drones and robots.

Rainwater Harvester Simulation

A comprehensive spreadsheet model of a rainwater harvesting solution for a remote community on Vancouver Island.
